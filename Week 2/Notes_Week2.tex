\subsection{Brief Intro - Brown Representability Theorem}
Let $h\mathcal{C}$ denotes the homotopy category of based spaces, then a functor $F:h\mathcal{C}^\Op\rightarrow \Ab$ is \emph{representable}, if there exists a based space $Y\in h\mathcal{C}$, such that $F\cong [\_,Y]$
